# Sale Tracker

> A focused iOS app for tracking leads, deals, commissions, revenue, and sales performance.

Sale Tracker is currently under active development. It is being built for sales professionals, freelancers, and small businesses that need a fast, practical view of their pipeline without the overhead of a traditional CRM.

## Why Sale Tracker?

Many sales tools are designed for large teams and require extensive setup. Sale Tracker is intended to make the essentials simple: record opportunities, see what is closing, understand commission, and stay focused on the next action.

## Planned capabilities

- Lead and deal tracking
- Revenue and commission tracking
- A lightweight sales-performance dashboard
- Goal tracking and reporting
- Cloud sync and team collaboration
- Integrations with popular business tools

## Project status

This is a pre-release project; it is not yet available on the App Store and has no public usage or download metrics. The immediate goal is a polished App Store release, followed by feedback-led iteration.

## Getting started

The current source is a SwiftUI starter structure. To develop the app:

1. Create or open the `SaleTracker` project in Xcode.
2. Add the source folders in this repository to the app target.
3. Build with a current iOS SDK.
